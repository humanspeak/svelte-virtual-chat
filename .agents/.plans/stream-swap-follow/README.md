## Findings considered and rejected

- Modifying `chatScrollPolicy.ts` to special-case replacement turbulence:
  rejected up front — the policy was hardened across recent releases with a
  dense spec net (scroll-escape, follow-drop, wheel-scroll-jump, scroll-away)
  and the suspected root cause is measurement identity, not scroll policy.
  The plan makes touching it a STOP condition rather than a strategy.
- Pruning orphaned height-cache entries in the same change: deferred —
  real but independent (memory growth over long sessions), and mixing it in
  would widen the blast radius of a P1 bug fix.
